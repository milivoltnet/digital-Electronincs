# digital-Electronincs
Digital Electronics repository: logic gates, combinational &amp; sequential circuits, flip-flops, counters, multiplexers, decoders, adders, FSM, Verilog/VHDL examples, K-maps, projects &amp; cheat sheets. Perfect for students, exam prep &amp; hobbyists.

# Digital Electronics

A well-organized, constantly growing collection of digital electronics resources, circuits, truth tables, Verilog/VHDL examples, projects, and study notes.

## Contents
- **01_Basics**              – Number systems, Boolean algebra, logic minimization (K-maps, Quine-McCluskey)
- **02_Logic_Gates**          – All basic & universal gates
- **03_Combinational_Logic** – Adders, subtractors, multiplexers, demultiplexers, encoders, decoders, comparators
- **04_Sequential_Logic**     – Latches, flip-flops (SR, D, JK, T), registers, counters
- **05_Finite_State_Machines** – Moore & Mealy machines, state diagrams
- **06_Memory**               – RAM, ROM, PLA, PAL basics
- **07_Verilog_VHDL**         – Ready-to-use code examples and projects
- **08_Projects**             – Traffic light controller, digital clock, vending machine, etc.
- **Cheat_Sheets**            – Quick reference tables and formulas

Perfect for:
- University/School coursework
- Exam preparation (midterms, finals, GATE, university entrance exams)
- Interview preparation
- Self-learners and hobbyists
